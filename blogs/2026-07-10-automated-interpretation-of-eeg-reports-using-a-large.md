Background: Free-text EEG reports typically lack structure, hindering scalable analysis. We evaluate a large language model (LLM) pipeline to extract structured diagnostic labels and confidence levels from these reports. Methods: We developed a hierarchical annotation schema to classify EEG reports for four specific abnormality types using a four-point confidence scale.
