Pedalling cadence during an acute eccentric cycling exercise altered physiological and perceptual responses. We examined the influence of cycling cadence on neuromuscular adaptation induced by a 6-week eccentric cycling training period. Eighteen participants performed training (eighteen sessions) at a cadence of 30 or 60 rpm over six weeks.
