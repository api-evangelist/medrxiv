Background: Nuclear medicine and radiopharmaceutical development require coordinated radiochemistry, dosimetry, molecular imaging, radiation-safety and clinical decision processes. Current workflows remain fragmented, difficult to audit and poorly standardised for evaluating domain-specific AI support. Methods: We developed RadGuide AI, a nuclear medicine agent built around a traceable data-model-tool loop.
