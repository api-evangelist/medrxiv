Rationale: Infections due to Extended-spectrum {beta}-lactamases-producing Enterobacterales (ESBL-PE) require empirical treatment with carbapenems. ESBL-PE carriage is considered as a risk factor for ESBL-PE involvement during ICU infection. Our aim was to determine factors that may predict the actual involvement of ESBL-PE. Methods: A two-periods bicentric ambispective study including ICU ESBL-PE
