Background/Objectives: Traumatic Brain Injuries (TBIs) often cause profound functional impairments, yet the influence of TBI mechanisms on stair-climbing functional independence over extended timelines remains poorly understood. This study assesses whether Rasch-transformed FIM Stairs scores varied by TBI mechanism over follow-ups spanning 10 years or more. Methods: Data from the TBI Model Systems database were analyzed.
